Biography

A Legacy of Courage & Constitutionalism

James Orengo is one of Kenya's most prominent legal minds and political figures. Having been at the forefront of the struggle for multi-party democracy in the 1990s, he has consistently championed the cause of the people.

His career spans decades of public service, from his early days as a firebrand Member of Parliament to his tenure as a Cabinet Minister and Senator, and now serving the people of Siaya as their second Governor.

Senior Counsel of the High Court of Kenya
Key Architect of the 2010 Constitution
Human Rights & Civil Liberties Defender
